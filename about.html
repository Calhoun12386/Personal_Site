Backpacking

Lost in the embrace of the Sierra Nevada's rugged majesty, I find solace and exhilaration in every step, every ascent. Having etched my footprints along the storied trails of this magnificent range, from the iconic John Muir Trail to the breathtaking High Sierra Trail, I've come to know these mountains like the back of my hand.

With the crown jewel of Mt. Whitney conquered numerous times, its summit a testament to both endurance and reverence, and the sheer granite face of Half Dome embraced in the light of dawn, I've danced with altitude's thin air and reveled in its challenge.

Yet, it's in the quieter moments, like those atop Mt. Darwin, where the world falls away, and I'm left with nothing but the whisper of wind and the beating of my own heart at elevations soaring beyond 14,000 feet. Here, amidst the grandeur of the Sierra, I find not only adventure but a deep communion with the soul-stirring power of nature.

Architectural Glass and Metal Worker

In the intricate world of architectural metal and glass, I navigate as a CNC programmer for a commercial glass company, sculpting precision and innovation into every piece destined to grace the skyline. From hospitals to schools and office buildings, my craft intertwines functionality with aesthetics, shaping the very structures that define our urban landscapes.

It's the marriage of creativity and problem-solving that fuels my passion for this work, where each blueprint and fabrication drawing serves as a canvas for my ingenuity. Armed with the advanced capabilities of machines like the Quadra and M4 from Emmegi, I orchestrate the symphony of multi-axis machining, milling and cutting, transforming metal stock lengths into the intricate components that form doors and windows.

Through meticulous attention to detail and a keen eye for design, I breathe life into the visions of architects and engineers, elevating the artistry of construction one programmed part at a time.
